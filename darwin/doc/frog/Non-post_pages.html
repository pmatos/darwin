3 Non-post pages

You can put .md, .mdt, and .scrbl files in "_src/" and its subdirs — except "_src/posts/" — and they will be converted to HTML pages.

For example, "_src/About.md" will be "/About.html" in the site.

Non-post pages are not included in any automatically generated index pages or feeds. You can manually add links to them in the nav bar by editing that portion of your Page template.
